Daisy the Great

Net worth

Daisy the Great's net worth is unclear.

Biography

Daisy the Great is an indie rock band. Their music contains themes of growing up and light-hearted absurdism. Their songs focus mostly on the vocals of Kel and Min, their lead singers. The music tends more towards a raw, unfiltered sound with little autotune or complex editing.

The Oh Hellos

Accomplishments:

  • Their most famous song is "Soldier Poet King"
  • The Oh Hellos are actually a brother and sister
  • They have released two EPs and eight albums

Net worth

About $5.09 million

Biography

The Oh Hellos have a style that's been described as "majestic" folk pop with a celtic influence. The first song they ever wrote together was for their mother's birthday. Their music has themes of adventure with an emphasis on the beauty of nature.

AJR

Accomplishments:

  • Their most famous song at the moment is "The World's Smallest Violin"
  • Jack, the band's youngest member and lead singer, was only 12 when the band began recording music and was still in highschool when they started to get famous
  • As of now, they have released five albums

Net worth

Something like $9.69 million

Biography

AJR is a modern pop band with a recognizable, if somewhat chaotic, sound. Their music focuses mostly on themes of romance with a sarcastic and goofy edge to their lyrics. The main members are Aron, Jack, and Ryan Met(formerly Metzger) are brothers.

Dragonforce

  • Their fastest song to date is "The Game", but their most famous is "Through Fire and Flames"

Net worth

About $45.1 thousand

Biography

Dragonforce is a speed/power metal band that has existed since 1999. They are famous for their extreme speed and skill. Their music is heavily inspired by video games and fantasy litterature.

Cosmo Sheldrake(that is his given name!!) is a singer-songwriter as well as a producer and composer. His music is exceedingly whimsical and frivolous with lullaby and nursury-rhyme like lyrics. It often encoperates sounds from nature and small trinkets like a coin rolling across a table.
